### Version 1.0.0 (Initial Release)
############################################
############################################

- Added 4 new Quests Types
- Fortify Area Quest requires you to fortify an area in the wilderness and defend from a small horde
- Loot Explosves will lead you to a POI where you need to find explosives and that unlocks:
- The Raze Building Quest where you need to blow up a super infested POI
- The Desperate Defence Mission will you require to clear an infested POI and defend from an extra big attacking horde after the timer runs out
- All Quests have 6 tiers
- SCore will give additional features but is not required
- Compatible with Darkness Falls
- Compatible with my Perk Mod
- I recommend using this with a mod that allows for more Mission Options 
- Should work in MP and be shareable, but could not test everything
- Big Thanks to CautiousPancake of the Trader Quest Addendum Mod for the inspiration
